Account Management

Delete Your Account

You can request full deletion of your Lions-X account and all associated data at any time.

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your progress, XP, LP, rank, and workout history will be permanently deleted.

01

Send us an email

Send a deletion request to support@lions-x.com from the email address associated with your Lions-X account.

02

Include your account email

In your email, confirm the email address you used to register. Use subject line: "Account Deletion Request"

03

We process your request

We will confirm receipt within 48 hours and complete the deletion within 30 days in accordance with GDPR.

Request Deletion

Contact Support

Send your deletion request directly to our support team.

Email Us to Delete Account

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • All XP, LP points and rank progress
  • Your complete workout history and training sessions
  • Your profile information and selected country
  • All app activity and analytics data tied to your account

What Is Retained

  • Anonymised, aggregated statistics that cannot be linked back to you (e.g. total app workouts completed) may be retained for product analytics.

Changed Your Mind?

  • If you submitted a request but wish to cancel it, contact us within 30 days at support@lions-x.com before deletion is completed.
