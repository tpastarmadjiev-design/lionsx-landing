Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Summary: Lions-X uses your camera only for real-time movement detection. No video is ever recorded or stored. We collect only the data necessary to run your account and track your fitness progress.

01

Who We Are

Lions-X ("we", "us", "our") is a fitness gamification application that uses AI-powered pose detection to track exercises and award rank points. Lions-X is operated by Lions Group.

Contact: support@lions-x.com

02

Data We Collect

When you use Lions-X, we collect the following types of data:

  • Account information: Email address and password (encrypted) used to create and manage your account.
  • Fitness data: Exercise type, repetition counts, XP points earned, LP (Lion Points), rank level, workout session timestamps and duration.
  • Location (optional): Country of origin, selected voluntarily by you for leaderboard display. We do not collect precise GPS location.
  • App activity: In-app interactions such as screens visited, training sessions started and completed, and app events used to improve the product.
  • Device information: Device type and operating system, used for technical support and crash diagnostics.
03

Camera Use

Lions-X uses your device camera exclusively for real-time AI pose detection. This means:

  • No video is ever recorded, stored, or transmitted.
  • Camera data is processed entirely on your device in real time.
  • No images or video frames are sent to our servers or any third party.
  • Camera access is only active during an active training session.

Your privacy is protected: Lions-X is safe for all ages. Parents can be confident that no camera footage is ever captured or stored by our app.

04

How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account
  • To track your workout progress, XP, LP and rank
  • To display your position on global and regional leaderboards
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To send important account-related communications

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

05

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud infrastructure provider. All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. Passwords are stored using industry-standard hashing.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be removed from our systems within 30 days.

06

Third-Party Services

Lions-X uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • MediaPipe (Google) — on-device pose detection (no data leaves your device)

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

07

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to request deletion of your data
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@lions-x.com or visit our account deletion page.

08

Children's Privacy

Lions-X is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

09

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or by email. Continued use of Lions-X after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
